About Us

Sewa Hi Sewa is a complete healthcare solution for everyone. Being the first hospital with a medical shop providing 63% off on branded medicines, we aim to bring health facilities for everyone at affordable prices. Our expert team of doctors has expertise in various fields providing the patients with world-class services. Our very own path lab offers all kinds of tests at affordable prices. Sewa hi Sewa enables healthcare solutions like dermatology, optometry & ophthalmology, gynaecology, physiotherapy, orthopaedics, neurology, and dental care. At present, we have two dental chairs with proficient dentists and free consultation. Also, our newly launched eye-care facility has cataract surgery at zero cost. In the near future, Sewa hi Sewa will also offer knee joint surgeries at cost-efficient prices.
